List of genres the app can detect :

  • Blues
  • Classical
  • Country

  • Jazz
  • Metal
  • Pop
  • Reggae
  • Rock

  • Music genre detection
  • Custom music genre detection
  • Music playlist creation

  • Keras 2.2.4-tf
  • Flask 1.1
